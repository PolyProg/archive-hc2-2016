The 2016 edition of the Helvetic Coding Contest is scheduled for March 19th.

Registration opens on February 15, and closes on March 5 23:59 CET (or when there are no more seats available). Participation fees are 5 CHF per participant.

We advise you to arrive no later than 10:00, so you won't miss on any of the breakfast. We unfortunately cannot wait for participants who arrive late.

Schedule

9:30-10:30 - Check-in

-- Breakfast buffet

10:45 - Welcome address / Sponsor talk / Technical introduction
12:00 - Dry run & clarifications

-- Lunch Buffet

13:15 - Contest start
17:45 - Contest end

17:50 - Poster session with solutions
18:15 - Diploma ceremony
19:00 - End
19:30 - Dinner (optional)
