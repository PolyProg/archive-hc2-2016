Registration for HC2 2016
You can work on your own laptop if you wish to (you can use one laptop per team and you will have to boot our live system). Note, though, that online participation is not possible, all participants have to physically come to EPFL.
Registration fees are 5.00 CHF per contestant.

The organizers will try to form balanced 3-person teams (to the best of their abilities) out of all individuals and 2-person-teams.

The submission deadline is 8 hours after the corresponding registration deadline. If you fail to meet the deadline your team will be put on the waiting list. Teams will be removed from the waiting list in the order they solve the task.
Contestant 1
We will use your e-mail address exclusively for communication relating to this and next year's contest. We will not transmit your e-mail address to our sponsors without your explicit consent.

Are you a member of VIS (computer science students association)? If yes, please change your affiliation to VIS for a discount. If you are studying computer science and payed your dues to VSETH, then you are also a member of VIS.

Members of VIS, and contestants of SOI (Swiss Olympiad in Informatics) can get their ticket reimbursed up to the Halbtax rate (VIS: up to 73.00 CHF). Please consider buying a 1-day travelpass from your commune instead (~44 CHF).
If you graduated during the fall semester last year, and are not currently working in a IT-related company, you may still register as a student.
The number of T-shirts per size is limited, but we'll try our best to give you a T-Shirt of a size as close as possible to your choice.
The after-contest dinner will take place in Restaurant Chez Xu, near the Lausanne train station.
If you answer yes, we will buy a metro ticket for you.
